🏗️ Database Setup

Lovable Assistant stores your data in your own private Supabase database. This ensures complete privacy and data ownership.

✨ Why your own database?
• Complete data privacy and ownership
• No central data collection
• Free Supabase tier (up to 500MB)
• Full control over your information

📖 Quick Setup Guide

  1. Go to supabase.com and create account
  2. Create a new project (free tier works perfectly)
  3. Go to Settings → API in your project
  4. Copy the "Project URL" and "anon public" key
  5. Paste them above and click "Connect Database"
  6. Extension will automatically set up the required tables
💡 Your database credentials are stored locally and never shared
